Render Keep-Alive Service

Welcome to our Render Keep-Alive Service! This platform is designed to help you keep your Render web services active and running smoothly.

Render provides a free tier that sleeps after 15 minutes of inactivity. Our service sends automated pings to your Render applications to prevent them from sleeping, ensuring your services stay online and responsive.

By using our platform, you can:

  • Keep your applications running 24/7
  • Customize ping intervals to match your needs
  • Monitor multiple services from one dashboard
  • Receive status updates and alerts
